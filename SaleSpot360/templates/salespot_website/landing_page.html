Our Features

Location Tracking

Keep track of your team with real-time GPS. Logs every movement, confirms store visits, and suggests smarter routes.

Attendance

Staff check-in with GPS and biometrics. Prevents proxy, tracks shifts, ensures compliance and accurate timekeeping.

Task Management

Assign tasks, track progress live, and connect seamlessly with CRM. Stay on top of deadlines and improve sales flow.

BEAT Plan

AI-powered route planning with auto-scheduling, real-time adjustments, and customer visit insights.

Expenses

Log expenses instantly via receipt scan. Auto-categorizes spends, enforces policy, and speeds up approvals.

Orders & Targets

Convert quotes offline, track sales targets, auto-calculate commissions, and prevent overselling.
