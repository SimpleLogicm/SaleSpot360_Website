About SaleSpot

SaleSpot is a smart and simple field sales app built for industries like FMCG, retail, and distribution. It helps your team stay organised, take orders, plan visits, and collect feedback – all in one easy-to-use app. With offline access, real-time updates, and multiple language support, your team can work smoothly, wherever they are.

It makes everyday sales tasks quicker – from booking orders to tracking performance – while cutting down on paperwork. Whether you're just starting or growing fast, SaleSpot scales with your business to boost productivity, improve customer experience, and drive results.
